Multilingual Servers

At ThirtyPixels, we believe in building a global Minecraft community where players from all over the world can connect and enjoy the game together. Our multilingual servers are designed to support players of various languages, offering an inclusive environment with localized game modes, chat support, and community events. Whether you speak English, Spanish, French, German, or any other language, you’ll find a space where communication and collaboration thrive seamlessly.

Thats all good, but how do I actually join one? Thats easy!
All you need to do is navigate to the "Multiplayer" tab in the Minecraft Java Edition Client. Once you are in that menu, you can either do a Direct Connection (join the server) or Add the server to your server list. Once you are in the menu, it will ask for a Server Address.
